About KAZOOMERANG

A small-town band with a hip, new sound.

Inspired by the Black-Eyed Keys, Alanis Moricassette, and the many pairs of sunglasses of Gwen Stefani.

We started our group one sumer night while watching an old re-run of Bonanza. Little Joe was in some big trouble with a gang of bandits. Scared, we hid beneath our hand-knitted blankets and waited as the most suspenseful moment of television history unfolded. At the worst possible time, the power went out. Driven mad from being deprived of this critical cinematic moment, we decided to start this band.
